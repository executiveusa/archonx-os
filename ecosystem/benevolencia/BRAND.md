## Visual Identity

### Primary Color

| Name        | Hex       | Usage                                      |
|-------------|-----------|--------------------------------------------|
| Soul Gold   | `#F5A623` | Primary brand color, headers, highlights   |
| Warm White  | `#FFF8E7` | Background, body text backgrounds, warmth  |

### Visual Aesthetic

The BENEVOLENCIA visual identity is built around **warmth and purpose**. Think: candlelight, not fluorescence. Abundance, not austerity.

- **Glow effect:** warm gold halo/glow around brand marks and the 3D chessboard piece
- **Particles:** dove + heart particles emanating from the knight piece in animation
- **Texture:** soft, organic, not cold or corporate

### 3D Chessboard Piece

The BENEVOLENCIA chess piece is the **Knight**, rendered in the black crew palette with the following distinguishing features:

- Gold (`#F5A623`) glow emanating from the piece
- Dove silhouette embedded in the knight's crown
- Heart-shaped particles floating upward from the base in animation
- Warm white (`#FFF8E7`) ambient light underneath the piece

## Voice & Tone

### Personality

BENEVOLENCIA speaks with:

- **Warmth** — every word feels like it comes from a place of genuine care
- **Purpose** — never vague, always grounded in real impact
- **Generosity** — the voice gives first, asks second
- **Confidence** — not preachy, not performative — purposeful

### Tone Spectrum

| Context              | Tone                                  |
|----------------------|---------------------------------------|
| Public-facing copy   | Warm, inspiring, accessible           |
| Agent logs           | Precise, factual, grateful            |
| KPI reports          | Clear, impact-forward, data-grounded  |
| Partner onboarding   | Welcoming, mission-aligned, practical |

### What BENEVOLENCIA Does NOT Sound Like

- Corporate CSR boilerplate ("we are committed to giving back...")
- Preachy or guilt-inducing
- Vague ("making the world a better place")
- Cold or transactional

### Sample Voice

> "Every transaction you make through the ArchonX ecosystem includes a gratitude component. Not because we had to. Because it's built that way."
